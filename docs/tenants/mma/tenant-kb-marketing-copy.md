- **Headline:** Your Personal AI Funding Advisor.
- **Sub:** Built for entrepreneurs serious about building wealth. Paige analyzes

  all in one conversation.

  fits the goal, which bureau the lender pulls, live prime-rate-based
  qualification, distance to the next approval threshold.

  working capital, CAC/LTV, BRRRR real estate financing.
- **Programs** — 8(a), WOSB, HUBZone, Veteran; SBA/CDFI/FDIC lender search;
  categorized lender database with bureau matching.
- **Elite** — assigned PME consultant; Mogul Credit AI coordination for
  disputes; priority funding application review.
