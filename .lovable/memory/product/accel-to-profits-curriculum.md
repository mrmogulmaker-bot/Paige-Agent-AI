**Tagline:** *From DIY Disputes to 7-Figure Credit Empire — The Complete Legal, Technical & Business Blueprint.*

Source of truth: `pme-knowledge-base.ts` Section 14.

## Learning Flow (sequence is non-negotiable)
1. Legal Setup → 2. Compliance Boundaries → 3. Tools & Methods → 4. Monetization → 5. Launch Strategy → 6. Service Delivery → 7. Growth → 8. Resources

## Modules
1. **Legal Foundation & Entity Setup** — LLC/Corp/Non-Profit, CROA bonding, banking infrastructure, repair-vs-report entity separation.
2. **Compliance & FTC Framework** *(taught BEFORE tech stack)* — CROA rules, pre-consultation disclosures, FTC red flags, GLBA Safeguards Rule.
3. **Tech Stack & Dispute Systems** — DisputeFox/CRC/SaaS, Metro 2 vs FCRA letters, advanced methodologies, small-claims FCRA litigation.
4. **Revenue Streams & Monetization** — Bizee formation income, monitoring/builder affiliates, broker partnerships, pricing tiers.
5. **Progressive Launch: DIY to Pro** — Fix own credit first, friends/family beta, team & systems, education layer.
6. **Fundability Engineering** — ACCEL protocol, banking architecture, BUILD Personal ascension, mortgage/auto prep.
7. **Scaling Systems** — Team/delegation, partner networks, content marketing, holding-company structure.
8. **Architect's Toolkit** — Contract templates, dispute letter libraries, CRM automation, financial advisory expansion.

## Paige Operational Rules
- **Gate Module 4 behind Modules 1–2 completion.** Selling without entity + CROA disclosures = #1 FTC trigger.
- **Require proof of personal results before Module 5 launch** (personal 720+ FICO + 3+ funded business credit lines).
- **Never recommend CPN / file-segregation / piggybacking-for-sale** — bright-line FTC violations (Module 2 L3).
- **Pair Module 3 letter methods with FCRA/FDCPA citations** from KB Section 4.
- **Coach persona detection:** if user mentions "my clients", "running a credit repair business", "starting a CRO", or "becoming a credit consultant" → surface this curriculum and offer next uncompleted lesson.
